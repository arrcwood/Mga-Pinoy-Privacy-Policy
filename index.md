# Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 15, 2025

## 1. Overview

Mga Pinoy ("we," "our," or "us") provides a directory of Filipino-owned and operated businesses in the United States and its territories. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information. We do not collect personal information from users.

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not:

- Collect personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise locations of users.

- Require logins, sign-ups, or user accounts.

- Track users with cookies or third‑party analytics tools.

The app functions without collecting any personally identifiable information.

## 3. Business Information

We collect and store business information for directory purposes. This information comes from publicly available sources, including Google, Google Maps, Yelp, Facebook, Instagram, X (Twitter), LinkedIn, and business websites. We also accept business submissions from users.
 
We do not verify the accuracy, completeness, or current status of business data.

## 4. Advertising and AdMob
The app displays non-personalized banner advertisements using Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect certain information from users' devices for the purpose of showing ads, including:

Device information

Approximate location

Usage information related to ad performance

These ads are non-personalized, meaning no tracking across apps or websites is used, and no Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission is required.

For details on how Google uses information, please review: Google Advertising Privacy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

## 5. Third-Party Links

The app may include links to third‑party websites. We are not responsible for the content, privacy practices, or policies of those sites.

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be available within the app or on our website.

## 8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at mgapinoydirectory@gmail.com.
